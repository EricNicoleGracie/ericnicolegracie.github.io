What should I wear?
Please dress formally. And bring a rain coat, just in case!

What time should I arrive?
The doors will open at 2pm, the ceremony will start at 2:30pm.

Do you have a registry?
We are grateful for your presence at the wedding, so gifts are not expected.

Is there free parking at Golden Gardens Bathhouse?
Yes, but it is limited (first come-first serve), so carpool or Uber/Lyft if possible, especially if you plan on drinking.

Can I bring my kid(s)/a plus one?
Due to the venue’s constraints, we are limited to a certain number of guests. Only those listed on your invitation can be accommodated. All guests must be 21+ years old.

When is the RSVP deadline? December 31, 2017

Is there a reception afterwards?
Yes, dinner and dancing to follow the ceremony. Dinner will be a buffet with vegan/gluten-free options available.

When does the reception end?
The reception ends at 9:30pm.

Will there be alcohol?
There will be an open bar during the reception. Please bring your ID, if you plan on drinking.

Can I take home the centerpiece at my table after the reception?
Yes, please do.

What should I do while I’m in Seattle?
  • visit the MoPop (Museum of Pop Culture)
  • visit the Seattle Aquarium
  • take the ferry to Bainbridge Island
  • go to the top of the Space Needle
  • canoe on Lake Washington at the UW WAC
  • kayak on Lake Union
  • take the Seattle Underground Tour
  • take a boat tour of Lake Union or the Puget Sound
  • visit the Living Computer Museum
  • visit the MOHAI (Museum of History and Industry)
  • visit Pike Place Market
  • visit the Seattle Art Museum
  • get ice cream at Molly Moon’s
